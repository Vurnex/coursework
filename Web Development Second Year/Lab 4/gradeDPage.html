Your grade was a D.

It's clear that some effort was put in at least. Here are some ways you can improve:

  • Work out where you’re falling short. Figure out the areas in which you’re underperforming, and why.
  • Talk to your teachers. Ask them where they think you need to improve, and they’ll probably have some advice on how you can go about it.
  • Improve your note-taking skills. Notes should be readable, useful / relevant, logically organised.
  • Find the right learning style for you. You might find it easier to work with a friend or two, so that you have someone else there to motivate you.
  • Stop procrastinating. This is a common response to a big workload, but only delays the inevitable!
